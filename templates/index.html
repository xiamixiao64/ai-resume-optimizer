Frequently Asked Questions

What is ATS? ▼
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies to filter resumes before human review. If your resume isn't ATS-friendly, it gets rejected automatically — no human ever sees it.
How does the AI optimization work? ▼
Our AI analyzes your resume against the job description, identifies missing keywords, rewrites bullet points with stronger action verbs, and restructures content for maximum ATS compatibility. Results in 30 seconds.
Is my resume data stored? ▼
No. Your resume is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We don't save, share, or analyze your personal data. Your privacy is our priority.
Can I use this for any job? ▼
Yes! Our AI works for any industry — tech, finance, healthcare, marketing, and more. Paste the job description for best results, and the AI will tailor your resume to match.
