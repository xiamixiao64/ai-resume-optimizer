Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free plan? ▼
The free plan includes 3 resume optimizations with ATS scoring, keyword matching, and ATS type detection. It's perfect for trying out the tool before committing.
Can I switch between plans? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to all features. When downgrading, your current billing period will complete.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay through our secure payment processor LemonSqueezy.
Is my data secure? ▼
Absolutely. Your resume is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We use industry-standard encryption and comply with GDPR regulations.
What's the difference between Pro and Lifetime? ▼
Pro is a monthly subscription at $9.9/month. Lifetime is a one-time payment of $149 that gives you permanent access to all current and future features. Lifetime saves you over $100 compared to 12 months of Pro.
