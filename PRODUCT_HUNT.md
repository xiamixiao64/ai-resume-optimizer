## Description (产品描述)
75% of resumes are rejected by ATS before a human sees them. ResumeForge AI analyzes your resume against real ATS filters, identifies missing keywords, rewrites bullet points with stronger action verbs, and generates tailored cover letters — all in 30 seconds.

🔥 **What makes us different:**
- ATS Score: Get scored 0-100 against real ATS algorithms
- Keyword Match: Find exactly what's missing for your target role
- Smart Rewrite: AI-optimized bullets & content
- Cover Letter: Generate tailored cover letters in seconds
- PDF Upload: Just upload your resume, we handle the rest

Free to start. 3 optimizations included.
